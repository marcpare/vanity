ray trace

a ray tracing engine built from the ground up in MATLAB. Mostly an experiment to see how far I could get.

story

Ray tracing—a technique for 3D rendering based on how a beam of light travels—turns out to be reasonably straightforward to implement. Count off each pixel in an image, generate a beam of light from an imaginary eye, and see what happens as it shoots through a scene.

I was learning MATLAB and linear algebra at the time. This project turned out to be a great testbed for both. To turn a scene, multiply by a rotation matrix. MATLAB is well suited for this sort of calculation.
