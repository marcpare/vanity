rough draft

an independent film shown at the Georgia Tech finale for Campus Movie Fest.

inspiration

A writer faces the reality of how his art fits into the equation of business. His struggle with identity collapses when his book deal falls through. Does he overcome it?

Primarily a chance to play with filming technique as a reflection of the character's internal state.

the team

My good friends Kento and Keyan reconnect each January by staying up all night for a week making a film for Campus Movie Fest. We've been shown every time we've entered; this was our latest work.

Keyan focuses on story and music direction, Kento on camera work, and I fill in the rest of the holes. In this piece, I orchestrated the prop work that made the filming in one continuous shot possible. There are 6 people in the room with Keyan; it was quite an interesting bit of choreography to keep them out of the frame.
