Some of the places I've been in the last few years. A sample of photos doesn't really do justice to the experience of travel, interaction with culture, or the serenity of nature. Maybe, though, you've been to some of the places I have, and we can share a conversation.

Banff | Sao Paulo | Cairo | Europe | Paris

Paris, France

My home for 5 months while at an internship with AREVA, Nuclear Power. Not really a trip, more like a dream: I lived in the neighborhood that Amélie was set in.

My apartment was up that street. Four friends are helping me carry a couch up the flights of tiny stairs. And that's Montmartre in the background.
