about the title

not wandering was the title of my undergraduate travel blog. It meant something about a conscientious approach to travel: a flaneur rather than a wander.

The mechanical engineer has a privileged look into today's big problems: energy, transportation, built landscape, and climate change. In other words, the design of almost everything around us can be influenced deeply by the mechanical engineer. In the spirt of not wandering, I think we can ask a lot more from the technical artifice that surrounds us. I design and build to demonstrate this point.
