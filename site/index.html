about the title

not wandering was the title of my undergraduate travel blog. It meant something about a conscientious approach to travel: a flaneur rather than a wander.

Mechanical engineering principles deeply influence the design of almost everything around us. These principles lie at the root of today's big issues: energy, transportation, built landscape, and climate change. In the spirit of not wandering, I try to ask a lot more from the surrounding technical artifice through design and development.
